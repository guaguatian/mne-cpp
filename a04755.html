[in]centerfreqdetermines the center of the frequency - normed to sFreq/2 (nyquist)
[in]bandwidthignored if FilterType is set to LPF,HPF. if NOTCH/BPF: bandwidth of stop-/passband - normed to sFreq/2 (nyquist)
[in]parkswidthdetermines the width of the filter slopes (steepness) - normed to sFreq/2 (nyquist)
[in]sFreqsampling frequency
[in]fftlengthlength of the fft (multiple integer of 2^x)
